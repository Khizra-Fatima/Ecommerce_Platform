Support

Welcome to the Support Page! We’re here to help you get the most out of our platform, whether you’re a customer or a seller. Below are some frequently asked questions and resources to assist you. If you still need help, please feel free to reach out directly to our support team.

1. Account & Profile Management

  • How do I update my account information?
    You can update your personal information, contact details, and other profile settings by navigating to the Account Settings section in your dashboard.
  • How do I delete my account?
    If you wish to delete your account, please ensure that any pending orders are completed and all products are removed (for sellers). Then, visit the Account Settings section and follow the deletion steps provided.

2. Order & Delivery Information

Customers:

  • Tracking My Order:
    You can track the status of your order in your Order History section, where you’ll find the estimated delivery date and current status.
  • Delivery Options:
    During checkout, you’ll be able to choose your preferred delivery option, such as Standard or Express.
  • Order Issues:
    If you experience any problems with your order (e.g., incorrect or damaged items), please contact the seller directly, or use the Report Issue button on your order details page.

Sellers:

  • Managing Orders:
    Go to the Orders section of your dashboard to view, process, and update order statuses. Be sure to update the delivery status promptly to keep customers informed.
  • Handling Returns:
    Ensure that your return policy is clearly stated in your store details, and respond promptly to any customer inquiries regarding returns or exchanges.

3. Product Listings (For Sellers)

  • How do I add or update my products?
    Go to the Product Management section to add new listings or update existing ones. Make sure your product descriptions, prices, and images accurately represent each item.
  • Why isn’t my product showing in my store?
    To make products visible, ensure they’re published and set to “available” status. Draft products or those with restricted availability won’t appear in your store listing.

4. Payments & Refunds

Customers:

  • Refunds:
    If a refund is approved (based on the seller’s return policy), you’ll see the funds returned to your original payment method within the specified processing time.
  • Secure Payments:
    All transactions are encrypted for security, and we never store sensitive payment information.

Sellers:

  • Receiving Payments:
    Funds from sales are deposited into your account after orders are marked as delivered. Please ensure your bank details are up-to-date.
  • Processing Refunds:
    To issue a refund, go to the order details page and follow the refund process. Refunds must comply with your store’s return policy.
